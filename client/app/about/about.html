Mission

Rockridge is an open source software project that demystifies the process
of building a personal financial plan. We empower and educate users
to take control of their financial futures.

Details

Rockridge is a simple, clean, intuitive web application where users answer a few questions to build a personal financial plan.
Financial simulations are performed behind the scenes to create the best plan for that particular user. These calculations,
based on years of combined experience and research of our team members, are reliable and accurate.*

Drive

Why did we create Rockridge? We saw firsthand how broken the personal financial advising industry is. Many
individuals are wary of financial advisors - and for good reason. Rockridge is a safe, zero pressure environment
that guides a user along the path to financial freedom and independence. Whether you are trying to
pay down debt, save for retirement, analyze your personal risk, or just understand your financial
situation better - Rockridge can help you achieve your financial goals. Rockridge doesn't cost
anything to use, but it can save you a lifetime of worry and stress.

Team

Rockridge is an open source project hosted on GitHub. Check it out, tell us what you think, share your expertise, and help make the world a better place.

*Rockridge can not guarantee the accuracy of any of its financial projections, as they are based on statistical averages.
